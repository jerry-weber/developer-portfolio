About Me

Hey there, I'm Jerry Weber.

As a Front-End Web Developer, I specialize in the art of creating engaging and accessible interactive websites. My journey into the world of coding began as a personal passion in 2017, but the initial spark was ignited back in 2008 when I joined MySpace and discovered it empowered users to customize their own pages.

The discovery that coding serves as a gateway to advanced communication, innovation, and collaboration ignited my imagination. Drawing from a background in music, I derive immense satisfaction from participating in creative and collaborative projects. I'm genuinely excited about the opportunity to channel this enthusiasm into your organization!

When I'm not immersed in the world of coding, you'll often find me strumming my guitar or delving into my extensive music collection, which includes vinyl, CDs, and digital tunes. I also enjoy surfing the web or, occasionally, multitasking by combining all three passions at once.
