About Me

Hello, I'm Jerry Weber, and I'm not your typical Front-End Web Developer. I see web development as an art, a way to craft engaging and accessible websites that connect with people. My coding journey began in 2017, but the seed was planted back in 2008 when I first customized my own MySpace page. It was like discovering a magic door to a world of endless possibilities.

Coding, to me, is more than just lines of text; it's a gateway to creativity, innovation, and collaboration. With a background in music, I find joy in working on creative and collaborative projects. I'm excited about the chance to bring that enthusiasm to your organization.

When I'm not deep in code, you'll often find me strumming my guitar or diving into my extensive music collection, which spans vinyl, CDs, and digital tunes. I also love surfing the web or, every now and then, juggling all my passions at once.

So, let's make something amazing together!
